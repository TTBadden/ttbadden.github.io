Artist Statement

As a multimedia designer, I essentially interested in creating work that catches the someone's attention. My artworks embodies concepts of self love along with depression and isolation. The color schemes that my work consists are different shades of pruple, blue, pink, green, yellow and orange. Where the dark shades represents isolation and depresssion within ourselves, in society. Where as the brighter shades represents our love and hope. It conveys that where there's light, there's dark. And where there's dark, there's light. Similiar to the Yin and Yang concept.The thing about art is that you can let your creavtivity led the way. It's a different form of expression that allows you to speak your mind.

My design is inspired by Malika Favre. She is a French illustrator and graphic artist. Her art style can be described as 'Pop art and Op art'. The way she uses colors inspires me to experiment with color and to challenge myself more
