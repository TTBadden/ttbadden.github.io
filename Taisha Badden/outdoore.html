Go Cloud-Watching

Lying on your back and watching the clouds dift away can be so relaxing. It's almost like meditation. You don't need to have a planned day, you can just simply go to the park or the roof top and watch the clouds.

Riding a bicycle is both beneficial for the body and mind. The body because your exercising the body muscles and the mind because the cool breeze hitting your face can be the best part. Just make sure to your bike through the park to be at peace with nature.

Take a stoll outside to make your mind clear from anxiety, stress and/or depression. Simply walking around either in the city or the park and looking around is great for your mindset.
